Ayurvedic

Ayurveda, a traditional system of medicine originating in ancient India, is a holistic approach to health and wellness that has been practiced for thousands of years. This system is deeply rooted in the belief that the mind, body, and spirit are interconnected, and it seeks to balance and harmonize these elements to promote overall well-being. At the core of Ayurveda is the concept of doshas, which are unique energy types that govern an individual's physical and mental characteristics. There are three primary doshas: Vata (air and space), Pitta (fire
