Common Health Concerns

1. Heart Disease

Causes: High cholesterol, high blood pressure, smoking, sedentary lifestyle

Effects: Heart attacks, heart failure, cardiovascular problems

2. Diabetes

Causes: Insulin resistance, unhealthy eating habits

Effects: Neuropathy, vision problems, cardiovascular issues

3. Obesity

Causes: Excessive calorie intake, lack of physical activity

Effects: Increased risk of heart disease, diabetes, joint problems

4. Mental Health

Causes: Genetics, life events, stress

Effects: Depression, anxiety, emotional difficulties

5. Cancer

Causes: Genetic mutations, exposure to carcinogens, lifestyle factors

Effects: Affects different parts of the body,
